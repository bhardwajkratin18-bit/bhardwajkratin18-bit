# Kratin Bhardwaj

**Senior Product Designer · Design Engineer** — Denver, Colorado

I design interfaces and ship the production code behind them. 6 years of end-to-end product design experience, with depth in AI-native products and design systems.

At FRCC I lead design across web, AI, and editorial surfaces and shipped 2 production AI products: a live chat assistant and an LLM-powered routing platform that routes with 91% accuracy, cut support triage from 15 minutes to 2, and won $300K in production funding on executive approval. My portfolio site, its design system, its 3D character cast, and its grounded AI assistant are all my own code.

Both live as case studies on my portfolio.
